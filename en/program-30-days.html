Contents

What's inside the guide

Written simply and clearly — no jargon, no "perfect poses". Every practice stands alone and is easy to follow even if you've never done yoga before.

Week 1–2

Foundations & grounding

Body scan, basic asanas for back and hips, introductory breathwork. Focus: calming the nervous system.

Week 2–3

Rhythm & breath

Pranayama techniques — nadi shodhana, kapalabhati, ujjayi. Morning and evening routines. Focus: energy and concentration.

Week 3–4

Strength & mobility

More dynamic practices — standing poses, warrior sequence, spinal rotation. Focus: steadiness and strength without strain.

Week 4

Integration & yoga nidra

Full practices (asanas + pranayama + meditation) and three guided yoga nidra sessions for deep relaxation and sleep.
