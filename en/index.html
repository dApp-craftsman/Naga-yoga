Behind the name

Who teaches

Stoyan Topalov

I am a teacher of authentic classical yoga — the kind that has been practiced for centuries, with a deep focus on the body, mind, and spirit.

My journey to India in 2020 divided my life in two. The first half was filled with the pursuit of ambitions, fleeting pleasures, and inner suffering. The second — with a deep search for truth, lasting happiness, and liberation.

My dharma (mission) is to pass on to the people around me the necessary tools for fulfilment and genuine meaning in life.

NĀGA YOGA

The nāga — in the yogic tradition, a symbol of quiet, grounded strength. The name reflects what I believe practice should feel like: something that moves with you, not against you. Here we do not chase progress — we create the conditions for it to arrive on its own.
